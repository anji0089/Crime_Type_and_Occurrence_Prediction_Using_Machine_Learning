Crime Type and Occurrence Prediction

The intentional commission of an act usually deemed socially harmful or dangerous and specifically defined, prohibited, and punishable under criminal law.

The Crime Type and Occurrence Detection system is a powerful platform designed to aid law enforcement agencies, researchers, and communities in predicting and understanding crime patterns. By utilizing advanced data analytics, artificial intelligence, and historical crime data, this platform aims to provide insights into the types of crimes likely to occur in specific areas and the factors influencing their occurrence.
